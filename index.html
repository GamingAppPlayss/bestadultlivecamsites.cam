Understanding Credits, Tokens and Pricing

One of the most common questions from new users is how the payment systems on cam sites work. Each platform has its own currency, but the underlying mechanics are similar across the industry.

Token Systems — Used by platforms like Chaturbate, Stripchat, and BongaCams. You purchase tokens in bundles (e.g., 100 tokens for $10.99). Tokens are then spent to tip performers, activate interactive features, or pay for private shows at a per-minute rate denominated in tokens.

Credit Systems — Used by sites like LiveJasmin and Flirt4Free. Credits function similarly but may be denominated in closer-to-real-dollar values, making pricing feel more transparent. Private shows are typically charged per minute in credits.

Free Browsing — Nearly all top cam sites allow free browsing of public shows with no account or payment required. Creating a free account typically unlocks additional features like saving favorites, sending chat messages, and receiving promotional bonus tokens.

Budget tips: buy token packages during promotional periods for the best value, take advantage of free token offers on registration, and start with free public shows to find performers you enjoy before spending in private sessions.
